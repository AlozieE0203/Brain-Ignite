Lemon flavour

Spark your senses with Brain Ignite's Lemon Fusion soda! Our zesty citrus blend delivers a refreshing burst of energy, combining sweet and tangy notes for a crisp, invigorating experience. Ignite your taste buds and refresh your mind with every sip!
