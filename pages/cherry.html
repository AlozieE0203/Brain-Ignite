Cherry flavour

Elevate your taste experience with Brain Ignite's Cherry Bliss soda! Our rich cherry infusion delivers a burst of fruity delight, combining sweetness with a hint of tartness for a refreshing experience. Ignite your senses and indulge in the succulent flavor with every sip!

Ingredients

Carbonated water, high fructose corn syrup, citric acid, natural and artificial flavors, cherry juice concentrate, sodium benzoate (to preserve freshness), potassium sorbate (to preserve freshness), caffeine, gum arabic, sucrose acetate isobutyrate, red 40.
